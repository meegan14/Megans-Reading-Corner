My favorite “book boyfriend” is Dorian Havilliard—he’s just utter perfection!

Discovering My Love for Reading

I started reading chapter books in kindergarten. I loved Purple Princess Wins the Prize by Alyssa Crowne—the pages of the book were purple, it was so cool! Growing up, I used to be so excited (and still am) to receive books for my birthday or holidays. I remember being in first grade and receiving Junie B. Jones books by Barbara Park for Easter—I read them immediately!

One of my all-time favorite book series was My Sister the Vampire by Sienna Mercer. I just thought the character Ivy on the cover was cool. The series was about twin sisters—one a vampire, the other human—and it reflected the bond I share with my own twin sister. Another favorite series from elementary school was the Waterfire Saga, which is all about mermaids and kingdoms at war. Both definitely sparked my love for fantasy books.

I read a couple different books in middle school since they still held the scholastic book fair for us. Some memorable books were The Secret Language of Sisters by Luanne Rice, Whenever I’m With You by Lydia Sharp, P.S. I Like You, and By Your Side by Kasie West, as well as Girl Stolen, Count All Her Bones, and The Girl I Used to Be by April Henry. I read a lot of other random things online especially in highschool that people around my age had written since I couldn't really buy books for myself.

Around my Junior year of highschool I got a job and could also drive. This changed the game completely. I remember going to Barnes & Noble with my sister and seeing the book Dance of Thieves by Mary E. Pearson sitting on a table, I bought it since it seemed pretty interesting. The moment I read that book I fell in love with reading all over again. Going to Barnes and buying books from then on became pretty constant leading me to where I am now, years later, with a bookshelf full of books.

Other Hobbies/Interests

Other than reading, I indulge in other hobbies. I can crochet—I’ve made cardigans, scarves, hats, book sleeves, and many other things. It’s something I picked up and taught myself. I can also knit, but I prefer crocheting. I used to play ukulele and guitar, and though I still have both, I don’t pick them up as often. I also enjoy drawing and painting—I keep a sketchbook and often make art for others as gifts.

I’ve played Minecraft my whole life—it was a huge part of my childhood and teenage years. I also grew up with a Nintendo DS Lite and later a Switch Lite, where I played Animal Crossing and Zelda: Breath of the Wild—my all-time favorite game. Zelda became a big fixation for me, as I learned its lore and timeline during 2020-2022. I also enjoy Fortnite, which is a fun way for me to connect with my nephews.

My favorite music artists of all time include One Direction, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, Niall Horan, and Taylor Swift—basic, but I’ve listened to them since middle school, and I’m okay with that!
