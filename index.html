Background: Wild Love is the highly anticipated first book in Elsie Silver's brand-new Rose Hill series. This book follows Ford who is Willas brother from her series Chestnut Springs, and a new character Rosie. In a need for money Rosie asks Ford who's her older brother's best friend to hire her so she can help him out with his daughter and Rose Hill records leading to a rekindling friendship and romance between the two.
